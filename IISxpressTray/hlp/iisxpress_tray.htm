The IISxpress Status Window allows you to monitor and change IISxpress to meet your needs. The IISxpress Status Window will be installed in your system notification area, to launch it double click on the icon or right click over the icon and select 'Status' from the menu.

If the IISxpress Status Window is not running in your notification area then you can run it from the IISxpress item in the Windows start menu.
